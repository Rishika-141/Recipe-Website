Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk powder (full fat)
  • All-purpose flour
  • Ghee
  • Baking powder
  • Milk (whole milk)
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom pods
  • Water
  • Optional: Rose Water

Process to make Gulab Jamun

  1. In a bowl, mix milk powder, all-purpose flour and baking powder.
  2. Gradually add melted ghee and milk, kneading until a smooth dough forms.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for about 30 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into small pieces and roll them into smooth, round balls.
  5. In a saucepan, combine sugar and water.
  6. Bring to a boil, stirring until sugar dissolves completely.
  7. Add cardamom pods, crushed, and simmer until the syrup reaches a one-string consistency.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • 3 cups milk powder
  • 3/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup + 3 tablespoons milk
  • 3 tablespoons butter or ghee
  • 1/2 to 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Process to make Barfi

  1. Grease a 9x6 baking pan.
  2. Heat a heavy bottom pan on medium to low heat.
    Once hot, add in the butter and 1/2 cup of milk. Let the butter melt.
  3. Once the butter melts, add in the milk powder stirring constantly.
    Once the powder is incorporated with milk, gradually add in the powdered sugar.
  4. Let the burfi set for at least three hours.
    Once set, it can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to seven days.
